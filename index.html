Gambling is where you bet something for a game of chance. For example, you can bet on a dice roll or a roulette spin. Below are some examples of gambling. (They are external links)

Poker is a game played with chips. Each player receives 2 cards from the dealer. The starting player then puts in 1 dollar for a small blind and the next player puts in 2 dollars the big bling. The dealer then places three cards that could make a special combination using the players 2 cards. After going around and putting in bets, using check (don't add anything), raise (raise the amount added to the pile), or call (match the amount raised). There are many types of combinations in the following order from least to most: High card (K to A, largest to smallest), one pair (two of a kind), two pair (two two of a kind), three of a kind (triple), straight (the numbers make a 5 in a row acsending by 1), flush (5 cards of same suit), full house (3 of a kind and 2 of a kind), 4 of a kind (quadruple), straight flush (straight with all cards same suit), and 5 of a kind (only possible with a joker).
